THE ENGINE

Maracania.AI isn't built on gut feeling. It treats football as a complex stochastic process, derived from millions of data points and simulated through rigorous statistical frameworks.

Monte Carlo Simulation

We don't predict the future once; we predict it 1,000,000 times. By simulating the remainder of the season a million times, we account for variance, luck, and chaos. The probabilities you see (e.g., "34.2% chance to win La Liga") represent the exact percentage of those million parallel universes where that outcome occurred.

Understanding the Metrics

Strength of Schedule (SoS)

A dynamic rating (0-10) of a team's upcoming opponents. We calculate the weighted strength of the next 5 opponents, accounting for Home/Away advantage. High (>6.5) indicates a "Nightmare" run-in, while Low (<3.5) indicates a favorable schedule.

Performance Delta
